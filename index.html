My pets are my family and I have a special place in my heart for each one of them. As our pets get older and sometimes develop illnesses, we are reluctant to leave them. We want to always stick around and watch them.

Am not sure if you are feeling me; we just want to be arond them,feed them or just make them feel special. Has someone who makes you happy gone dull for just an hour? God! It always feels like forever, you just want them to get back to normal. I am committed to providing you not only the the reassurance, but the confidence that you need, to feel comfortable leaving your pet.

I run Happy Pets on a simple philosophy, whatever your pet needs, I will take care of it while you are gone because I want the same for my pets.
